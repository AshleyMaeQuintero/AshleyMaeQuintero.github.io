Hello, I'm Ashley

A photographer
who genuinely cares.

I'm a California-based photographer driven by the belief that documentary photography is the most honest kind. I don't arrange people into scenes — I follow them through their day and pay attention.

When I'm not shooting, you'll find me hiking with my dog, chasing down estate sales for old film cameras, and drinking too much horchata. I'm endlessly grateful for the couples who trust me to tell their story.
